About Me

Hi! My name is Prashanti and I am currently a rising sophomore at Carnegie Mellon University in the School of Computer Science. I am a computer science major and plan to do a concentration in Algorithms and Complexity as well as either an additional major or minor in math. I love problem solving, algorithm design, discrete math, and typesetting proofs (its so satisfying!).

I am currently a STEP intern at Google with the Surveys team. I also am a teaching assistant at CMU. I was a TA for 15-122 (Principles of Imperative Computation) for Spring 2020 and will be a TA for 15-251 (Great Theoretical Ideas in CS) in the fall 2020 semester. My responsibilities for 15-122 included grading assignments, teaching a lab, and designing written assignments. I have also previously been involved with robotics through FRC and VEX.

In my free time I play lots of board/card games (Avalon, Settlers of Catan, Dominion, and more!), slowly make my way through the local library's science fiction and fantasy section, and waste time on Discord.
